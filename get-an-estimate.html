How accurate is this, really?

A range, not a number.

It's an honest ballpark — wide enough that we'd stand behind it, narrow enough to plan around. We'd rather show you a range up front than a precise number that quietly balloons later. A real scoping conversation is what turns this into a fixed figure: we pin down the features that matter, the ones that can wait, and the integrations hiding in the details.

Get a tighter number

What tightens the range

  • A clear, prioritised feature list
  • Examples or references you like
  • Known integrations and platforms
  • Existing designs or brand assets

What widens it

  • Scope that's still taking shape
  • Design starting from scratch
  • Undecided third-party systems
  • Compliance or regulatory unknowns
