Straight answers

Wondering if you fit?

Not at all. We work across the full curve — from pre-revenue founders to regulated enterprises. What changes by stage is the emphasis, the budget conversation, and the pace; the standard of work doesn't.

The thirteen here are where we go deepest, but they aren't a wall. We've almost certainly built something adjacent, and the engineering practices transfer. Tell us your domain and we'll be straight about how much of it we already know.

The five steps stay the same; the weighting shifts. Early-stage gets a compressed kickoff and a lean MVP scope. Enterprise gets extended scoping, more documentation, and tighter change control. Same backbone, different ceremony.
