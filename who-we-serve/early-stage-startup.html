Early-stage Startup

From napkin to product launch.

You've got an idea, a deadline, and a finite runway. We help pre-revenue founders validate it, design it, and ship an MVP you can put in front of users — and investors — without burning the budget on a gold-plated v1.

Get an estimate Schedule a call

What keeps you up at night

The worries every early-stage founder has.

We've sat across the table from enough founders to know exactly where the anxiety lives — and how to take it off your plate.

Runway is the clock

Every month of build is a month of burn, and the round won't wait.

→ We scope to a real budget and ship an MVP that can raise the next round — not a feature museum.

You can't vet engineers

Without a technical co-founder, you can't tell a good dev shop from a bad one until it's too late.

→ A senior team you don't have to interview, and straight advice instead of an upsell.

Move fast — but not into a wall

Speed now can mean a rewrite later, right when you can least afford one.

→ A lean MVP built on foundations that won't force a rebuild the week it starts working.

You're the founder, not the CTO

You need to make technical calls you're not equipped to make alone.

→ Plain-English updates and every trade-off explained, so you stay in control without a CTO.
