The healthcare problems we actually solve.

Get an estimate Schedule a call

What makes healthcare hard

The problems we see in this vertical, repeatedly.

Compliance is the price of entry

Ship without HIPAA in order and you don't ship at all — or worse, you ship and get fined.

→ We design the controls in from day one, not bolt them on before an audit.

Your data lives in legacy systems

The new product is worthless if it can't talk to the EHR, the lab feed, and the scheduling system.

→ We integrate via HL7 and FHIR, so the new layer speaks the language the old stack already does.

A breach is existential

Patient data getting out isn't a bug ticket — it's headlines, fines, and lost trust.

→ Encryption, least-privilege access, and audit logging as standard, not paid add-ons.

Clinicians won't use clunky software

If it adds clicks to a 12-hour shift, it gets worked around — and your rollout fails.

→ Workflows designed around how care actually happens, tested with real clinical users.
