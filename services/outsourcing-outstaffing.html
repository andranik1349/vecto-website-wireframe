Outsourcing & Outstaffing

Plug expert engineers into your team — fast.

Two ways to add senior engineers without the hiring grind: outsource a whole team we run for you, or outstaff — augment yours with people you manage directly. Same vetted talent; you choose how much of the wheel you hold.

Get an estimate Schedule a call

What's included

Two models. The only real question is who runs the team.

Most buyers don't know the difference until someone draws the line — so here it is, plainly.

We run it

Team Outsourcing

A full squad — engineers, QA, a delivery lead — that we staff and manage against your goals. You point at the outcome; we own the how.

Explore Team Outsourcing You run it

Staff Augmentation

Individual senior engineers who join your team and take direction from your managers — extra capacity in your process, not a separate one.

How we'd staff it

The model decision is the buying decision.

Each sub-service maps to exactly one engagement model — pick the way of working, and the rest follows.

Dedicated Team

How it works

Outstaffing

How it works

Straight answers

Before you add to the team.

Usually two to three weeks for a dedicated team, and faster for a single augmentation. We propose matched, pre-vetted engineers; you interview and approve everyone before they start.

It depends on the model. With Team Outsourcing, our delivery lead manages the team day-to-day against your goals. With Staff Augmentation, your managers run the engineers directly — we just handle employment and HR.

We plan for it. Knowledge is documented rather than siloed in one person's head, and we backfill a departing engineer with continuity — onboarding their replacement on our time, not yours.

All IP is yours, assigned in the contract from day one. NDAs and standard EU-compliant agreements are in place before any work begins — you own everything the team produces, full stop.
