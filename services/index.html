Yes — that's the norm. Most clients start at the stage they need most (often Build) and bring us into the stages before or after as the product grows. There's no requirement to buy the whole journey up front.

That's exactly where a single end-to-end partner earns its keep. Discovery feeds design, design feeds build, and the same team carries the context across — so nothing gets lost in a handoff between vendors.

Most don't fit neatly, and that's fine — the stages are a way to talk about the work, not a rigid package. Tell us the outcome you're after and we'll map the right mix. The estimator is a good 90-second starting point.
