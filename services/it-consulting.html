How a discovery engagement runs

Five steps to a confident go / no-go.

1 Brief Capture the idea, the goal, and the real constraints.
2 Research Market, users, competitors, and technical feasibility.
3 Frame Turn the findings into a clear product direction.
4 Validate Test that direction against real, observed demand.
5 Specify Hand over a spec, model, and estimate to build from.
