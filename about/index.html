About VECTO

The team behind every project we ship.

We're a product partner for non-technical founders and scale-ups — small enough that you'll know everyone working on your product, experienced enough that we've seen how it usually goes wrong.

Our story

Twelve years turning other people's ideas into products.

VECTO started as three engineers taking on the projects bigger agencies wouldn't — the ambiguous, early-stage ones where nobody had written the spec yet. That's still the work we like best. Today we're a team of forty across delivery, design, and AI, and we've shipped products founders have gone on to raise on, sell, and scale.

2014

Founded in Yerevan

Three engineers, a rented room, and a habit of saying yes to hard briefs.

2018

First dedicated teams

Scale-ups started embedding us long-term; we grew design and delivery around them.

2022

AI practice formed

A standing team for the question every founder now asks — and for talking them out of it when the answer is no.

Today

Forty people, one bar

Across three offices — still measuring ourselves by what actually ships.

Mission

Help founders build the right thing — not just any thing.

Most products don't fail on code; they fail because nobody wanted what got built. Our job is to spend your budget where it proves the idea, and to be honest about the parts that can wait.

Vision

A world where a good idea isn't blocked by not being technical.

Founders shouldn't need a CS degree to get a fair estimate, a clear plan, and software they can trust. We want to be the partner that makes the technical part legible.
