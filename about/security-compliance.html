Security & Compliance

How we protect your code, your data, and your IP.

This is our security posture — the practices and commitments we hold ourselves to on every engagement, not just the badges. Built for the procurement and InfoSec teams who need to sign off before we start.

Certifications

Where we stand today.

We name what we hold and what we're working toward — no vague "enterprise-grade" claims. Certifications are added here as they're achieved.

SOC 2 Type II
Held · since 2024

Independently audited controls for security, availability, and confidentiality. Report available under NDA.

GDPR
Compliant · ongoing

EU data-protection practices baked into how we store, process, and delete personal data — DPA available.

ISO 27001
In progress · target Q4 2026

Formalising our information-security management system; audit scheduled for late 2026.

HIPAA
In progress · target Q2 2026

Already delivered HIPAA-aligned builds; formalising BAAs and controls for healthcare clients.

NDA & IP ownership

Your idea, and your code, stay yours.

We sign your NDA — or provide ours — before the first detailed conversation. Everything we build for you is yours.

  • Mutual NDA signed before any scoping detail is shared
  • Full IP transfer on payment — code, designs, and assets assigned to you in writing
  • Code escrow available for longer engagements, on request
  • No reuse of your code or data to train models or seed other projects

Data handling

Where your data lives, and who can touch it.

We keep access to the minimum needed to do the work, in regions you approve, and we clean up when we're done.

  • Hosted in your chosen region (EU / US / UK) on your accounts where possible
  • Least-privilege access; production data restricted to named team members
  • Defined retention windows — we don't keep data longer than the engagement needs
  • Documented destruction of credentials and data at project close

Secure development

Security in the build, not bolted on after.

Code review

Every change is peer-reviewed before it merges. No direct-to-production commits.

Dependency scanning

Automated vulnerability and dependency scanning in CI, with alerts on known CVEs.

Secret management

Secrets live in a managed vault, never in code or config — rotated and access-logged.

Deployment isolation

Separate environments per stage; production isolated with restricted, audited access.

Compliance experience

We've delivered under real requirements.

Finance

Payments and fintech builds delivered with audit trails, data residency, and PCI-aware handling of card data.

Healthcare

HIPAA-aligned products with protected health data, access controls, and documented data flows.

Security review

We make your sign-off easy.

We've been through plenty of buyer-side security reviews and questionnaires. We don't slow them down — we come prepared.

1

Send us your questionnaire

SIG, CAIQ, or your own — we'll complete it, usually within a few business days.

2

Review our documentation

SOC 2 report, DPA, and policy summaries shared under NDA for your InfoSec team.

3

Agree controls in the contract

Any project-specific requirements are written into the engagement before kickoff.
