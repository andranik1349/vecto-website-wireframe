Straight answers

About our stack.

This list is what we reach for most, not the limit of what we'll work with. If your product runs on something we haven't listed, we'll tell you honestly whether we're a fit — and if a specialist tool is the right call, we'll say so rather than forcing our defaults onto your project.

Our deepest benches are in the React/Next.js + Node.js/TypeScript world and the .NET ecosystem, on AWS infrastructure — that's where most of our work lives. But specialisation cuts both ways: we won't pretend to be experts in something we're not, and we'll point you elsewhere if your need is genuinely niche.

We start from the problem, not the tech. The choice follows the product's needs, your team's ability to maintain it after we hand over, and how mature and well-supported the technology is — boring and proven beats shiny and risky for most businesses. We explain the trade-offs in plain English during scoping, so you understand why, not just what.
