What to expect

Thirty focused minutes.

No slideshow, no hard sell. We listen first, react honestly, and leave you with something useful whether or not we work together.

0–10 min

Your context

You walk us through the idea, where you are, and what's in the way. We mostly listen.

10–25 min

Open discussion

We react: what we'd build first, the risks we'd watch, roughly what it takes, and how we'd work together.

25–30 min

Next steps

A clear, no-pressure summary of options — and what a written proposal would cover if you want one.

What to bring

Nothing required. A little helps.

Come as you are — even a half-formed idea is enough to have a good conversation. If you have any of these handy, the call goes further.

  • A sentence or two describing what you want to build
  • Any existing materials — sketches, a deck, a competitor you like
  • What success looks like, and any deadline you're working toward
  • A rough budget range, if you have one in mind

What happens after

No follow-up you didn't ask for.

A written summary

Within 24 hours you'll get the notes from the call — what we discussed and any options we suggested — in writing.

No sales sequence

We don't drop you into an automated funnel. If you want to take the next step, you reach out — on your timeline.

No commitment

The call is genuinely free and obligation-free. Plenty of good conversations end with "not yet" — and that's fine.
